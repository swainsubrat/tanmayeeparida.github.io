This project studies the **structural and magnetic properties of double perovskites** Sr₂NiMO₆ (M = W, Mo). These materials are of interest due to their tunable magnetic and electronic properties relevant for spintronic applications.

Key findings:
- Crystal structure determination via X-ray diffraction
- Magnetic characterization (magnetization vs. temperature and field)
- Analysis of exchange interactions and magnetic ordering in the double perovskite structure

Published in *AIP Conference Proceedings* 3198, 020123 (2025).

**Techniques used:** XRD, Magnetometry, FESEM.

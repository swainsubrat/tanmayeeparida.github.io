This project investigates the **temperature-dependent transport properties** of the Fe₂TiSn Heusler alloy — a half-metallic material of interest for spintronics. The study combines structural characterization with electrical transport measurements across a wide temperature range.

Key findings:
- Crystal structure and phase purity confirmed by XRD
- Temperature-dependent I-V characteristics revealing transport mechanism transitions
- Analysis of carrier transport (hopping vs. band transport) at different temperature regimes

Published in *AIP Conference Proceedings* 2995, 020139 (2024).

**Techniques used:** XRD, FESEM, Temperature-dependent I-V measurements.

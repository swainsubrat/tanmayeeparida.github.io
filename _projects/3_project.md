This project is inspired by the **neuromuscular junction** — the biological synapse between a motor neuron and muscle fiber. The aim is to mimic mechanosensitive synaptic behavior using flexible hBN-based memristive devices for neuromorphic hardware applications.

Key contributions include:
- Design and fabrication of mechanosensitive hBN memristors on flexible substrates
- Emulation of neuromuscular junction behavior: plasticity under mechanical stimulation
- Demonstration of synaptic functions (EPSC, PPF, LTP/LTD) under combined electrical and mechanical inputs
- Potential for application in soft robotics and prosthetics

**Status:** Manuscript submitted.

**Techniques used:** CVD hBN, Flexible substrate processing, SMU-based pulse measurements, AFM, Raman spectroscopy.

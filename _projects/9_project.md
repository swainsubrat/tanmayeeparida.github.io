This page documents the **thin film deposition and device fabrication** expertise developed throughout my PhD and MSc research. Reliable control over film quality and interface engineering is central to all memory and neuromorphic device studies.

**Deposition Techniques:**
- RF and DC Magnetron Sputtering (HfO₂, TiN, metals)
- Spin Coating (organic films, sol-gel oxides)
- Thermal and E-beam Evaporation (metal contacts)
- Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) — hBN growth
- Pulsed Laser Deposition (PLD) — complex oxides

**Characterization:**
- Structural: XRD, XRR, TEM, FESEM, AFM, Profilometry
- Compositional: XPS, Raman, UV-Vis, Ellipsometry, EPR
- Electrical: SMU (Keysight B2902A, Keithley 4200SCS), Impedance Spectroscopy (EIS)

**Computation and Simulation:**
- Python, VASP, Origin, Gnuplot for data analysis and DFT simulations

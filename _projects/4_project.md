This M.Sc. thesis project (February – June 2021, Jawaharlal Nehru University) investigated the **structural and dielectric properties of complex transition metal oxides and Heusler alloys**, including Sr₂NiMO₆ (M = W, Mo) and Fe₂TiSn.

Key contributions include:
- Structural characterization (XRD, FESEM) of double perovskites and Heusler alloys
- Study of magnetic and transport properties at varying temperatures
- DC electrical measurements: temperature-dependent I-V characteristics
- Impedance analysis of dielectric behavior

This foundational work led to two conference publications in *AIP Conference Proceedings* (2024, 2025).

**Techniques used:** XRD, FESEM, DC I-V measurements, Impedance spectroscopy.

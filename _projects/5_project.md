This collaborative project develops a **flexible organic piezoelectric nanogenerator** with high power density, combining excellent ferroelectric properties and memristive characteristics in a single flexible device.

Key contributions include:
- Fabrication and characterization of organic piezoelectric thin films on flexible substrates
- Demonstration of high power density under mechanical deformation
- Multifunctional behavior: energy harvesting + non-volatile memory in a single device
- Characterization of ferroelectric hysteresis and memristive switching

Published in *NPG Asia Materials* 18(1), 4 (2026).

**Techniques used:** Spin coating, Thermal evaporation, Ferroelectric characterization, I-V measurements, PFM.
